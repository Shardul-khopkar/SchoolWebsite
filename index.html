Hi!

Sou A. K. Joshi School ranks 1st in Thane with SSC Board syllabus. All members of A.K. Joshi Parivar continuously work with full of dedication to impart quality education for the development and progress of the students. Since 25 years our SSC result is 100%.Our students Always secure good marks and percentage in this examination. Last 13 years our students are visiting China for presenting our science project for CASTIC i.e. China Adolescent Science and Technology Innovation Contest and every year we get award at National level!! Our students are also attending Belt and Road Teenager Camp for learning how to prepare science projects at China from last two years. Other than academics, students are encouraged to participate in different competitions and different examinations where they secure grand success.To develop their personality Parliament is formed for secondary section with different ministries. Head Boy and Head Girl are also elected every year. Different club activities with lot of programmes are arranged for Primary and Secondary sections. Syllabus for primary is decided according to the new pattern of education. Our teaching methods are different. Keeping the syllabus same, teachers are making teaching very interesting so that students gain lot of knowledge to be par with ICSE and CBSE syllabus. For Primary and K.G sections we started library period. Students are free to take library books at home.Primary and K.G students also get wonderful success in academic as well as non-academic fields.All methods for K.G are implemented based on ‘Play Way method’ concept. For English, Maths and Science subjects, many basic concepts are cleared in KG section only.

  • Safety and security

    The primary responsibility of every school is to ensure a safe and secure environment for students,staff and visitors.Keeping this in mind the school has provided unique Identity card to each student and staff. By using this card electronic attendance alongwith SMS to the parents is ensured.Thus students can be tracked from the moment they enter the school till they leave the premises. Considering the security of the students, CCTV cameras are installed in the premises.
